Issa Rae is bringing her voice to Google Assistant

You can hear Issa Rae answer your questions, tell you about the weather, offer words of inspiration and more, while the regular Google Assistant voice handles the rest. Just say “Hey Google, talk like Issa” to get started. After you make the change, listen up - you may hear some fun surprises along the way. Here’s a couple to get you started - just say “Hey Google, compliment me” or “Hey Google, can you beatbox?”
